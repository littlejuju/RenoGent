How you use it

No new app. Four steps in WhatsApp.

Renovation coordination in Singapore already lives in WhatsApp. So does RenoAI — contractors see a well-organized homeowner, never a bot.

1

Link once

Scan a QR from your phone — the exact same gesture as WhatsApp Web. That's the whole install.

2

Create your family console

A WhatsApp group for your household. Group membership is the permission system — either spouse can feed the agent and approve its actions.

3

Feed it your plans

Drop your floor plan and a one-line brief. Get back per-room renders, a verified structural fact layer, and green/amber/red compliance triage with verbatim HDB citations.

4

Supervise on autopilot

The agent watches your contractor group, logs every promise, chases every slip — and drafts the message. You reply ok to send it as yourself.

Renders you can verify

Every render is audited against your floor plan.

AI renders love to invent windows and stretch rooms. RenoAI doesn't get to: each render is hash-paired to a stamped copy of your plan showing exactly where the camera stands — then machine-audited in three ordered layers.

The three-layer audit — ① components: every wall, door, window, beam and column reconciled against a manifest traced from your plan; ② depth & scale: room proportions checked against the plan's printed dimensions; ③ style & HDB typology. Fail layers 1–2 and the render is re-edited or escalated to you — never silently shipped.

Capabilities

The agent executes. The human decides.

📐

Fact layer

Your floor plan becomes an immutable, metric-grounded ground truth: mm/px calibration from printed dimensions, per-room structural briefs, every overlay diff-checked against it.

🎨

Design & render

Structure-locked, per-room renders constrained by your actual walls, windows and HDB typology — generate → audit → surgical re-edit, in a loop.

👤 you sign off on taste
🚦

Compliance triage

Green / amber / red against a scraped hdb.gov.sg corpus. A citation gate rejects any rule quote that doesn't match the source verbatim — no hallucinated regulations.

🛒

Procurement that learns you

Contractor sends a 40-page catalog? You get a top-3 with pros & cons. After a few picks the agent learns your decision profile and pre-selects — you just confirm.

👤 you confirm every pick
📒

Supervision & the promise ledger

"Tiling done by Friday" gets logged the moment it's said. Slippage detection is mechanical; chase messages are drafted for you and sent as you.

👤 nothing sent without your ok
🔒

Privacy by whitelist

The agent subscribes to exactly two chats — your family console and your renovation group. Everything else is dropped at the event entry point: not read, not parsed, not stored.

🔐 The trust model

  • Rides your WhatsApp — no bot number, no platform in the middle
  • Family console group = access control; either spouse approves
  • Whitelist of 2 chats; all other conversations never enter the pipeline
  • Every outgoing message passes a human approval gate — by design, the automation channel physically cannot release a live message

⛔ The boundary that never bends

RenoAI is decision support. It does not replace licensed professionals:

  • Structural works — executed by HDB-registered contractors, under permit
  • Permits & submissions — licensed pros, always
  • Final sign-off on anything irreversible — the homeowner, always
