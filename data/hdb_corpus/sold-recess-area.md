# Sold recess area — HDB Renovation Guidelines for Building Works

| **Types of Building Work** | **Need Permit?** | **Subject to the following Guidelines/ Conditions** |
|---|---|---|
| All other renovation within sold recess area (e.g. installation/ replacement of grille/ gate/ door/ fixed awning/ roof/ windows/ floor and wall finishes, topping up of floor and demolition/ erection of non-load bearing walls, etc.) | Yes | Conditions/ guidelines will be given for the renovation item upon evaluation and approval |
